Currywurst

Description

A sliced bratwurst in sauce with curry powder, served with fries.

Ingredients

  • Bratwursts
  • 1 cup ketchup
  • 5 tsp. curry powder
  • (Optional) 1-2 teaspoons of other powders (paprika, pepper, onion)
  • 2 tsp. Worchestershire sauce
  • 4 tbsp. beef broth

Steps

  1. Pour ketchup on a saucepan on medium heat.
  2. Add the powders, then liquids, and stir well before simmering for 5-10 minutes.
  3. Pan fry the bratwurst, then cut and add sauce on it.
